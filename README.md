# 📖 Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive **Workforce Insights Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Excel** and **Power Query**.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce composition, employee demographics, department distribution, tenure analysis, employment status, and workforce trends. It enables HR leaders and management teams to monitor workforce health, analyze employee distribution, identify staffing patterns, and support strategic workforce planning.

The solution transforms raw employee data into meaningful insights, helping organizations make data-driven decisions regarding talent management, workforce optimization, and organizational growth.

As a **Business Analyst with 4+ years of experience**, I developed this project to demonstrate how workforce data can be transformed into actionable HR insights through interactive dashboards and KPI reporting.

# 🎯 Business Problem

Human Resource departments manage large volumes of employee data across departments, locations, and organizational levels. Without an effective reporting solution, management often struggles to:

- Monitor workforce composition
- Track active and inactive employees
- Analyze employee tenure distribution
- Understand workforce demographics
- Monitor department staffing levels
- Identify workforce trends
- Support succession and workforce planning
- Make informed talent management decisions

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a centralized workforce reporting and analytics solution.
